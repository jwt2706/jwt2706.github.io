A bit about me...

Hi, my name is Jacob.

Computers always seemed so cool to me, and trying to figure out how they work has been a passion of mine for years. It started out with Lego Mindstorms and Scratch. I joined an FLL (FIRST Lego League) team and participated in competitions for 6 years. Then I got too old to continue, but then I had found out about FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition), which was FLL but for high school students. I joined a community based team, Merge Robotics (2706), for 3 years. I was in a business-like team of 35+ other students to build a full, real life robot. During my time there, I've learned an incredible amount on how to work in a team, develop software, solve real world problems and much more.
Finally, I graduated high school and was awarded 'Le prix de la persévérence de l'Université d'Ottawa'.
